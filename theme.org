** Switching

The following places require updates to theme:

- alacritty: .config/alacritty/alacritty.yml
- emacs: .config/doom/config.org
- firefox
- gitui: .config/gitui/theme.ron
- slack
- tmux: .tmux.conf
- wezterm: .config/wezterm/wezterm.lua
